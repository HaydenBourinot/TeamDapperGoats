What is DGschool?

DGSchool and all of its content was created by five students from across Nova Scotia who collaborated virtually from their homes during the Covid-19 pandemic. DGSchool is designed for people who want to begin to learn to code so it begins at the basics. Each lesson consists of a video tutorial component as well as an interactive coding sandbox for you to try out what you’ve learned. Welcome to Dapper Goats School !
